"An elastic material formed usually by weaving, felting, or knitting natural or synthetic fibers and filaments," is the definition of fabric. In its simplest form, the fabric is a substance consisting of fibers that are used to create everyday goods such as clothing, footwear, bags, and home furnishings like bedsheets, pillows, and towels. The characteristics of various varieties of fabric depend on the material they are manufactured from; they can be thick or thin, rough or smooth, heavy or light.
